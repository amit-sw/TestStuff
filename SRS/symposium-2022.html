Keynote Speaker

Dr. Nikunj Oza

Role: Leader, Data Sciences Group, NASA Ames Research Center

Nikunj Oza leads the Data Sciences Group at NASA Ames and a NASA project team applying machine learning to aviation safety and operations. His research spans data mining, machine learning, ensemble methods, anomaly detection, and aerospace applications. He has published 50+ research papers and received multiple NASA honor awards.
