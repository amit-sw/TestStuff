Keynote Background

Keynote speaker background and program videos from the official 2024 symposium archive.

Keynote Speaker

Robert Ober

Role: Chief Platform Architect for Data Center Products, NVIDIA

Rob is NVIDIA's Chief Platform Architect for data center products. He works with global hyperscales to architect AI clusters and datacenters, systems and platform architecture, and influence NVIDIA silicon, systems, and software roadmaps. His background is in processor and system architecture with experience in storage, networking, and wireless.

Keynote Speaker

Surbhi Kaul

Role: Product and Engineering Leader (Google, YouTube, Netflix, Cisco)

With 20 years of experience leading product design, development, and strategy in Fortune 500 companies, Surbhi has built and led product and engineering teams at Google, YouTube, Netflix, and Cisco. Over the last decade she launched multiple AI and machine-learning-driven products, including query disambiguation and speech recognition efforts for Google Home and Google Assistant devices.
