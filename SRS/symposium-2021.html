Keynote Speaker

Dr. Bharath Ramsundar

Role: Founder & CEO, Deep Forest Sciences; Lead Maintainer, DeepChem

Bharath Ramsundar, PhD, is founder and CEO of Deep Forest Sciences and lead maintainer of the DeepChem open-source consortium for medicine discovery. He previously co-founded Computable and holds a PhD in Computer Science from Stanford University. His work focuses on deep learning applications in drug discovery.
