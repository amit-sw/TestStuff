Call for Student Presentations

Abstract Submission Form

Students in grades 6-12 can submit original AI projects. Keep your abstract concise and focused on the problem, approach, and expected impact.

Eligibility

Open to middle and high school students (grades 6-12) working on AI-powered projects across domains such as healthcare, climate, agriculture, social impact, and education.

Submission Deadline

Abstract submissions are due by April 30, 2026. Accepted presenters will receive follow-up guidance before the symposium date.
