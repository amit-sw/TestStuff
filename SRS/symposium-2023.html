AIClub 2023 Student Research Symposium

The 2023 symposium featured student AI research and a keynote focused on large-language-model foundations, architecture patterns, and practical future directions.

Previous: 2024 Next: 2022

Keynote Background

Keynote speaker background and program videos from the official 2023 symposium archive.

Keynote Speaker

Ashish Bansal

Role: ML Infrastructure Leader, Google; Author of Advanced NLP

Ashish works on accelerating ML workloads at Google. He has previously worked on recommendation systems and NLP at Amazon/Twitch, Twitter, and Capital One, and has founded startups. He is co-founder of the IIT AI/ML forum and, in this keynote, presented key architectural foundations behind LLM development and future directions.
